## The big lesson (from "Pop the Bubble")
- At our previous hackathon, **Pop the Bubble**, we were **too technical and got marketing traction too late.** We had little/no user traction to show.
- **This time: distribution-first.** Land a waitlist/landing page in hour 1, drive a single growing traction number all day, get real users at the venue. The build serves the traction, not the other way around.

## Why this strategy fits EF
- EF's thesis: **"Found, don't follow."** They back **founder potential, ambition, and execution** over a polished product or business plan.
- Implication: a simple app + real traction + a credible "this is a big company" story scores higher than a complex demo with no users. Our traction-first plan is aligned with how they judge.
